What Are Admonition Tags, and Where are They Used?

Admonition tags are used to give readers important reminders.

Commonly used admonition tags include the following:

  • Error Message

They are used to communicate errors, incompletion, or serious bugs on my side. They are not about the content of the page or post, but simply a reminder for myself about system errors and maintenances.

They are used to communicate meta-information about the post, such as special status of the post.
