Touhou Blooming Chaos 2

Description: Touhou Blooming Chaos 2 is a fun-filled, innovative 2D rogue-like game that combines RPG and FTG elements. In this game, you can control more than a dozen beloved characters to navigate through numerous randomized labyrinth, meet special room and friends on the way, defeat boss and collect items, palette, music and equipment.

Motivation: TBC2 is made in Unity3D. I took over this large project in my first full-time internship and developed it from its EA stage to full official release. It was a brilliant success in China indie game studios - up to now we have sold 60k+ copies and is still rated “Overwhelmingly Positive” on Steam. Here is my memorial of those memorable months of development.

The characters and the the map were from Touhou Project created by Zun. This is a commerical Doujin game. Want to know more about Touhou Project and our Doujin community? You can start here.

Status: Finished and published. I am no longer maintaining this project - it will be maintained by other developers in the studio, most likely interns.

Touhou Fairy Knockout

Description: Touhou Fairy Knockout is a multiplayer party game where ou will role-play as playful fairies with unique personalities, and accomplish your own Gekokujō in Gensokyo where the strong gather! Make your own favorite fairy character the strongest with your own hands!

Motivation: Touhou Fairy Knockout is a continuation of my last game jam work (Inconsistent Memories). I did not use the old codebase but instead rewrote all lower-level architecture. I also implemented networking over LAN. By hosting matchmaking and relay servers around the world I am able to deliver remote multiplay experience to players.

Status: Just released on 28 July 2023 as Early Access. Still working on it! Already purchased by 4000+ players as of 30 July 2023.
